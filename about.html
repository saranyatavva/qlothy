ABOUT US

Influenced by our modern way of living & rich culture, Qlothy is a fashion exploration for the collective modern people throughout World.To inspire, share and find joy in empowering & celebrating individual expression through fashion. Our design aesthetics are rooted in Indian sensibilities of craftsmanship with functionality, timelessness and quality at its core. The design is a flow of a story which is a journey itself.
We, at Qlothy, are inspired by this journey of discovery.

Our Inspiration

Qlothy is inspired by people's love for fashion.
When talking about the inspiration to start this brand, One of our founder says, “I always loved fashion, like any other girl. Growing up I could never afford fancy designer clothes. I have always wanted fashion accessible to everyone so they can celebrate their individual expression through fashion. Be it a college student or a working professional, there’s something for everyone at .
For me, Qlothy is a reflection of who I am, as I am.”
